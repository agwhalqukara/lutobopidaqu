Bruno Nogueira Overview and Wiki

Early Beginnings

Born in the heart of Lisbon, Bruno‘s journey began in 1982. Despite the lack of public information about his early education and family life, what’s undeniable is his early flair for the dramatic arts, culminating in his debut on the soap opera Anjo Selvagem in 2002.

Rising Fame

The year 2003 marked a pivotal turn in Bruno’s career. He not only hosted Curto Circuito but also delved into stand-up comedy. Shows like Levanta-te e Ri and Manobras de Diversão became stepping stones, showcasing his unique comedic style.

Establishing a Name

Perhaps the most significant phase in his career was the creation of Odisseia on RTP1 in 2013. This show, coupled with his acclaimed performance in Último a Sair, garnered him the prestigious Sociedade Portuguesa de Actores best actor award.

Diverse Ventures

Apart from his on-screen achievements, Bruno has been a staunch supporter of the LGBTQ community, earning him the 2010 Rainbow Award. His roles in various shows and movies like Sorte Nula have further cemented his status in the entertainment world.

Personal Life
